What is SDET?

SDET (SOftware development engineer in Test) is a member of Quality Engineering team who is a full stack QA. Unlike traditional QA SDET possess all testing skills needed to deliver a quality application. This includes functional and non-functional testing. SDET needs to have good understanding and hands on with programming languages and coding. Today many companies hire SDETs to write unit tests as well.
